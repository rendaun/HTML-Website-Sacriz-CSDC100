WHO IS

SILICA GEL?

SILICA GEL (실리카겔) is a South Korean boy band (formerly co-ed) that was formed in 2013 and debuted on August 21, 2015 with the EP Pure Sun, or 새삼스레 들이켜본 무중력 사슴의 다섯가지 시각 (Five Perspectives of a Weightless Deer Revisited). Their current line-up consists of Kim Geonjae, Kim Chunchu, Kim Hanjoo and Choi Woonghee. They have four former members: Goo Gyeongmo, Kang Donghwa, Lee Daehee and Kim Minyoung. They were under Bunga Bunga Records from 2013 to 2021, but moved to Magic Strawberry Sound in 2021. They were originally a performance group combining rock music and VJing.
